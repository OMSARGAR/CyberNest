About CyberNest

Our Mission

CyberNest was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to make virtual computing accessible to everyone, everywhere. We believe that the ability to run any operating system, on any device, without complex setup or expensive hardware, should be available to students, developers, and professionals alike.

What We Do

CyberNest provides a cloud-based platform that allows users to instantly launch and access virtual machines with a wide variety of operating systems, directly in their web browsers. Our technology eliminates the need for local virtualization software, complex configurations, or powerful hardware.

Our Technology

Built on a foundation of cutting-edge virtualization technology, CyberNest leverages containerization and advanced streaming protocols to deliver a seamless desktop experience. Our infrastructure is distributed across multiple global regions to ensure low latency and high availability.

Privacy Policy

At CyberNest, we take your privacy seriously. We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your virtual machines and data are encrypted and isolated from other users.

  • We collect account information (name, email) to manage your subscription and provide support.
  • Usage data helps us improve performance and user experience.
  • Virtual machine data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time.
